**Brand Core**
FIVE01 is a serious, competitive darts platform with arcade energy. The vibe is high‑performance, credible, and fun. The product feels like a premium league platform, but with the excitement and speed of an arcade cabinet.

**Positioning**
FIVE01 is the most complete online darts platform: AI training, ranked play, tournaments, leagues, and video‑verified integrity in one place.

**Brand Pillars**
- Performance first: ranked integrity, stats depth, verified play.
- Arcade thrill: fast onboarding, bold motion, rewarding feedback.
- Community: leagues, tournaments, seasons, progression.
- Global: works anywhere, any device, any skill level.

**Tagline Options**
- “Play Serious. Train Smarter.”
- “Online Darts, Evolved.”
- “Train. Compete. Rise.”
- “The Complete Darts Platform.”

**Voice & Tone**
- Confident, technical, and competitive.
- Short, action‑driven sentences.
- Avoid gimmicks or forced slang.
- Lead with outcomes: “Improve your 3‑dart average” vs “cool stats.”

**Logo Usage**
- Primary lockup: use on dark backgrounds where the mark is most legible.
- Clear space: at least the height of the “0” around the logo.
- Min size: 24px tall for in‑app icons, 64px tall for web headers.
- Don’t stretch, rotate, or add effects beyond subtle glow.

**Color System**
Core colors are already established in `app/globals.css`.
- Midnight (Background): `#070311`
- Ink (Muted): `#160F29`
- Primary Teal: `#14A5B8`
- Secondary Green: `#16A249`
- Foreground: `#E7E1EF`

Suggested accents to support modes and rank tiers:
- Competitive Gold: `#A38D5B`
- Arcade Magenta: `#E45BFF`
- Arcade Cyan: `#4CCBFF`
- Warning/Clutch: `#F59E0B`
- Critical: `#EF4444`

**Mode Themes**
Use color accents by context. Primary teal should remain the base signal color.
- Ranked/Competitive: gold accents, high‑contrast cards, less gradient noise.
- Arcade/Party: magenta/cyan gradients, bolder glow, faster motion.
- Training: blue/teal accents, clarity over spectacle.
- Social/Leagues: emerald/teal accents, warm highlights.

**Typography**
Current pairing is strong for “serious + arcade.”
- Display: Space Grotesk (headlines, hero, section titles).
- Body: Inter (paragraphs, UI labels, controls).
If you want a more arcade‑forward option later, consider adding “Oxanium” for display while keeping Inter for body.

**UI Signature Elements**
- Angular cards with soft radii (12px) and glassy overlays.
- Subtle grid/scanline background texture.
- Purposeful motion: parallax in hero, staggered reveals, hover lift.
- Glow used sparingly and only for primary actions.

**Brand Imagery**
- Real board footage and camera frames over stylized renders.
- Shots of ranked tiers and achievements for credibility.
- Use dark backdrops with teal/green edge lighting.
